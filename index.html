About me

Hi! I'm Fraser, a UK based software developer. My initial academic focus fell on mathematics and the sciences, which lead to my study of chemistry at university. During this time my focus shifted further towards computational chemistry and the use of simulation in research, ultimately developing these and broader programming skills especially throughout my masters research investigating the use of software in cutting edge luminescent compound research.

It was in the lay period between the cessation of my course and the issuing of results that I took time to investigate machine learning, web development and performance conscious software development.

Currently my focus is on my role leading the development of Biverb Ltd's NLP AI products. Alongside that I continue to work on novel software development and both front and back end web development for organisations of varying sizes.
